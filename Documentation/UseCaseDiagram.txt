USE CASE DIAGRAM - BANKING SYSTEM
==================================

ACTORS:
-------

1. Customer
   - Primary user of the banking system
   - Can perform banking operations on their own accounts

2. Administrator
   - Bank staff member
   - Has elevated privileges for system management
   - Can view all accounts and transactions

USE CASES:
----------

A. AUTHENTICATION & AUTHORIZATION
   UC-1: Sign Up
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: New customer creates an account with username, email, password, and personal information
      - Preconditions: Customer does not have an account
      - Postconditions: New user account created, customer can login

   UC-2: Login
      - Actor: Customer, Administrator
      - Description: User authenticates with username and password
      - Preconditions: User account exists and is active
      - Postconditions: User is logged in and redirected to dashboard

   UC-3: Logout
      - Actor: Customer, Administrator
      - Description: User logs out of the system
      - Preconditions: User is logged in
      - Postconditions: User session ended, redirected to login

B. ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
   UC-4: Create Account
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer creates a new bank account (Savings, Checking, or Credit)
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in
      - Postconditions: New account created with account number

   UC-5: View Accounts
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer views list of all their accounts with balances
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in
      - Postconditions: Account list displayed

   UC-6: View Account Balance
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer views current balance of a specific account
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, account exists
      - Postconditions: Account balance displayed

   UC-7: Update Profile
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer updates personal information (name, phone, address)
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in
      - Postconditions: Profile information updated

C. TRANSACTION OPERATIONS
   UC-8: Deposit Funds
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer deposits money into an account
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, account exists
      - Postconditions: Account balance increased, transaction recorded

   UC-9: Withdraw Funds
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer withdraws money from an account
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, account exists, sufficient balance
      - Postconditions: Account balance decreased, transaction recorded

   UC-10: Transfer Funds
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer transfers money from one account to another
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, both accounts exist, sufficient balance in source account
      - Postconditions: Source account balance decreased, destination account balance increased, transactions recorded

D. TRANSACTION HISTORY
   UC-11: View Transaction History
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer views all transactions for a specific account
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, account exists
      - Postconditions: Transaction history displayed

   UC-12: Filter Transactions
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer filters transactions by date range or transaction type
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, viewing transaction history
      - Postconditions: Filtered transaction list displayed

   UC-13: Generate Statement
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer generates monthly account statement
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, account exists
      - Postconditions: Account statement generated (can be printed/downloaded)

E. ADMINISTRATOR OPERATIONS (Optional)
   UC-14: View All Accounts
      - Actor: Administrator
      - Description: Administrator views all customer accounts in the system
      - Preconditions: Administrator is logged in
      - Postconditions: All accounts displayed

   UC-15: View All Transactions
      - Actor: Administrator
      - Description: Administrator views all transactions across all accounts
      - Preconditions: Administrator is logged in
      - Postconditions: All transactions displayed

   UC-16: Manage User Accounts
      - Actor: Administrator
      - Description: Administrator can activate/deactivate user accounts
      - Preconditions: Administrator is logged in
      - Postconditions: User account status updated

F. LOAN MANAGEMENT (Optional Enhancement)
   UC-17: Apply for Loan
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer applies for a loan through the system
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in
      - Postconditions: Loan application submitted

   UC-18: Check Loan Eligibility (AI-based)
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: System uses AI to check customer's loan eligibility
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, applying for loan
      - Postconditions: Eligibility result displayed

   UC-19: Track Loan Payments
      - Actor: Customer
      - Description: Customer views loan payment history and remaining balance
      - Preconditions: Customer is logged in, has active loan
      - Postconditions: Loan payment information displayed

RELATIONSHIPS:
--------------

- Customer <<includes>> Login before all banking operations
- Customer <<extends>> Sign Up (if new customer)
- Deposit, Withdrawal, Transfer <<includes>> Verify Account Balance
- Transfer <<includes>> Verify Destination Account
- Withdrawal, Transfer <<includes>> Check Sufficient Funds
- View Transaction History <<extends>> Filter Transactions
- Administrator <<generalizes>> Customer (inherits all customer use cases)
